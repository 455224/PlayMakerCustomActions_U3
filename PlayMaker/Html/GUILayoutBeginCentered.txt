<p>
Begin a centered GUILayout block. The block is centered inside a parent GUILayout Area. So to place the block in the center of the screen, first use a GULayout Area the size of the whole screen (the default setting). NOTE: Block must end with a corresponding GUILayoutEndCentered.</p>
