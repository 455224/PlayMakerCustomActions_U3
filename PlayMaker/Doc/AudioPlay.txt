<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Plays the Audio Clip set with Set Audio Clip or in the Audio Source inspector on a Game Object. Optionally plays a one shot Audio Clip.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Game Object</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The GameObject with an AudioSource component.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Volume</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Set the volume.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
One Shot Clip</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Optionally play a 'one shot' AudioClip. NOTE: Volume cannot be adjusted while playing a 'one shot' AudioClip.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Finished Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send when the AudioClip finishes playing.</div>
</div>
