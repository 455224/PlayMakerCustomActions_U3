<p>
Smooth animation from ortho ( or perspective) to Perspective. Use GameObject reference instead of camera reference</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Camera</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The camera to animate. If null or not defined, uses the MainCamera</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Fov</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The Field of View to reach</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Near</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The near plane distance to reach. Leave to none for no effect</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Far</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The near plane distance to reach. Leave to none for no effect</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Duration</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The duration of the transition animation</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Transition Done Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event sent when transition is done</div>
</div>
