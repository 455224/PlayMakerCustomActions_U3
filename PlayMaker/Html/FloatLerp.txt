<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Linearly interpolates between 2 floats.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
From Float</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
First Float</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
To Float</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Second Float.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Amount</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Interpolate between FromFloat and ToFloat by this amount. Value is clamped to 0-1 range. 0 = FromFloat; 1 = ToFloat; 0.5 = half way in between.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Store Result</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the result in this float variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if any of the values are changing.</div>
</div>
