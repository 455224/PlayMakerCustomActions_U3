<p>
Sends an Event when the mobile device is shaken.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Shake Threshold</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Amount of acceleration required to trigger the event. Higher numbers require a harder shake.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Send Event</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Event to send when Shake Threshold is exceded.</div>
</div>
