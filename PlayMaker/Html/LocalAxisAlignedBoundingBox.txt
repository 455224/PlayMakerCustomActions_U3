</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Mesh Target</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The Mesh to use to compute bounds from</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Bounds Size</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Store the result in a Vector3 variable.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
