<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Enables/Disables the PlayMakerGUI component in the scene. Note, you need a PlayMakerGUI component in the scene to see OnGUI actions. However, OnGUI can be very expensive on mobile devices. This action lets you turn OnGUI on/off (e.g., turn it on for a menu, and off during gameplay).</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Enable GUI</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Set to True to enable, False to disable.</div>
</div>
