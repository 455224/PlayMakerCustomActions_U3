<div id="actionDesc">
<p>
Creates a rotation which rotates from fromDirection to toDirection. Usually you use this to rotate a transform so that one of its axes eg. the y-axis - follows a target direction toDirection in world space.</p>
</div>
<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
From Direction</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
the 'from' direction</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
To Direction</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
the 'to' direction</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Result</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
the resulting quaternion</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Repeat every frame. Useful if any of the values are changing.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Every Frame Option</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Defines how to perform the action when 'every Frame' is enabled.</div>
</div>
