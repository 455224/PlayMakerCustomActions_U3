</div>

Id</div>

</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Transform Target</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Look at a transform position.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Vector Target</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
A target position the GameObject will look at. If Transform Target is defined this is used as a local offset.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Time</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The time in seconds the animation will take to complete.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Delay</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The time in seconds the animation will wait before beginning.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Speed</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Can be used instead of time to allow animation based on speed. When you define speed the time variable is ignored.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Ease Type</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
For the shape of the easing curve applied to the animation.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Loop Type</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
The type of loop to apply once the animation has completed.</div>
</div>

<div id="paramRow">
	<div id="paramName">
Axis</div>
	<div id="paramDesc">
Restricts rotation to the supplied axis only. Just put there strinc like 'x' or 'xz'</div>
</div>
